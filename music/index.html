Artist world

Brazil raised. Australia shaped. Nature in the bloodstream.

WELI’s stories move between southern hemisphere textures: countryside memory, immigrant reinvention, queer nightlife, academic corridors, and the emotional pull of nature. The sound can move from pop precision to Latin warmth, club rhythm, theatrical flair, and world-inflected groove — always with a strong point of view.

This is not just a discography page. It is meant to feel like an environment: somewhere people can stream the work, understand the artist, and immediately sense the premium, technological, music-first identity behind the project.

What sits behind the sound
