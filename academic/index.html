Academic profile

Research, science communication, and research-creation

Dr Weliton Menário Costa is an artist–researcher working across ecology, behaviour, and science communication, with a focus on translating research into culturally meaningful formats across music, visual storytelling, and public engagement.

Current research

Science communication, culture, and popular media

Bridging research and culture through creative and analytical work

Current collaborations with Dr Anna-Sophie Jürgens (CAPSA, ANU) explore how science is represented and experienced through popular culture and artistic practice.

This includes research on music as a form of science communication, and on theatrical and camp representations of science — including alien characters, exaggerated scientists, and speculative worlds — and how these shape public perception, belonging, and engagement.
