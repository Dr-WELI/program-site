Meet
Dr WELI

Facilitator · Behavioural ecologist · Recording artist

Behavioural ecologist designing cultural experiences, workshops, and programs through music, movement, and storytelling. People & Culture · Student Experience · STEAM & Wellbeing. Dance Your PhD winner (Kangaroo Time). Founder of WildWooHoo.

Profile

Brazilian born, Sydney based — building bridges between science, performance, and public culture.

WELI is the facilitator and creative behind the room. A behavioural ecologist with a PhD on kangaroo personality, he designs activations, workshops, and programs that pair scientific rigour with movement, music, and storytelling — so corporate teams, university cohorts, school groups, and creative collaborators leave energised, connected, and reflective.

The same lens powers WildWooHoo, his creative studio, where research becomes culture through music videos, film, and public moments. The flagship project, Kangaroo Time, won the 2024 Dance Your PhD competition and screened globally.

Sister practice · Creative production

The creative studio behind the work, connecting nature, culture, and people through music, film, and high-impact communication. Where research becomes culture.

Studio

Music · Film · Visual storytelling

WildWooHoo is a creative studio for the animal kingdom - including us. It expands the work beyond workshops into music, music videos, animated series, books, and public moments, drawing parallels between animal and human societies.

Kangaroo Time is a flagship WildWooHoo project and won the 2024 Dance Your PhD.
