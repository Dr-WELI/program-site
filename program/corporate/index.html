Workshop
Themes

Six themes · Drawn from animal behaviour, neuroscience, and team systems

Each one runs as a 60 to 120 minute workshop or a multi-session program. Pick the one that fits the conversation your team is actually having.

T.01

Mindset for engagement

Practical reframing of barriers, drawn from nature systems. Teams move from passive attendance to active contribution.

T.02

Teamwork and peer influence

Synchrony, cooperation, and conflict-repair principles from the science of social behaviour, applied to real groups.

T.03

Organising your brain

Lessons from neuroscience and social evolution. Workable models for stress, focus, and sustainable motivation.

T.04

Nature-inspired thinking

Collective animal behaviour as a guide to reducing hesitation and building group confidence under uncertainty.

T.05

Diversity, individuality, and coexistence

What ecology teaches about real difference at work, drawn from Kangaroo Time and behavioural research on personality, role, and group life.

T.06

Transdisciplinary thinking for innovation teams

Borrowed from the university catalogue and adapted for R&D, strategy, and product teams. Working across disciplines without losing edge.
