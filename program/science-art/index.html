Examples of work

These projects show how scientific ideas can be translated into cultural experiences that travel beyond traditional academic channels.

  • Kangaroo Time - animal behaviour research translated into music, movement, and storytelling
  • G.O.A.T. (Cabra das Cabras) - Brazilian research translated into cross-cultural performance work

Many of these projects are developed through WildWooHoo, the creative studio platform for nature-culture storytelling across music, film, and science communication.

Flagship example of science × art storytelling in a public-facing format.
